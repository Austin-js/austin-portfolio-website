My Story


I did not actually graduate from any computer science related course, and it was actually one of my regrets. Since I was a kid, I have always loved tinkering and creating scripts with my computer back then!

During the pandemic, I first took interest of Forex and crypto trading to try and make money. It was then I realized I could make scripts to automate our trading strategies.

I learned MQL4 and MQL5 to create our first original trading bots with my friends. We shared and sold it to other people, and it was a bit successful in the short term. We stopped because the bot was not entirely perfect. We didn't like the risk being given to our clients.

Eventually, I transitioned to trading cryptocurrency and I learned about the Ethereum blockchain. I found out I can automate transactions using Python and Web3. I developed bots that can snipe tokens during their launch. I started being a Freelancer on Fiverr and sold my services there.

For 2 years, I was working as a freelancer - being able to work with many clients. My work was mostly about automating transactions on the Ethereum blockchain. During this time, I learned a lot with my experiences on Python, took several different Python and CS courses, and did side projects to improve my skills. I was playing around with AI around this time too (regarding photo generation and chatbots). However, I had to stop freelancing on Fiverr when they restricted crypto-related services similar to my previous projects.

I transitioned by looking for a remote full-time job, and I was able to get a job as a Python/Backend Developer. On the side, I am also working on my own projects related to AI that would help businesses.
